Sound familiar?

You are looking forward to your vacation, but motion sickness (kinetosis) and discomfort soon cloud the journey. This unpleasant feeling arises from a sensory conflict: Your eyes report a standstill (e.g., when looking at a smartphone inside a vehicle), while your balance organ simultaneously feels the movement. The brain sounds the alarm.

This is exactly where Viativity™ comes in

Instead of just numbing the symptoms, Viativity™ uses smart, digital tools. The app helps your body resolve this contradiction, gently fade out disturbing stimuli, and calm your nervous system – scientifically based and completely without medication.
