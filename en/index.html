Sound familiar?

You are looking forward to your vacation, but motion sickness (kinetosis), seasickness on a cruise, or fear of flying soon cloud the journey. This unpleasant feeling arises from a sensory conflict: Your eyes report a standstill (e.g., when looking at a smartphone inside a vehicle), while your balance organ simultaneously feels the movement. The brain sounds the alarm.

This is exactly where Viativity™ comes in

With smart immediate relief and preventive training, the app helps your body actively resolve this contradiction. You learn to gently fade out disturbing stimuli and calm your nervous system – scientifically proven and completely without medication.

Digital Tools & Travel Tips

Choose the tool that best supports your current situation. Whether it's acute motion sickness on the go or preventive training beforehand: Viativity offers you the right solution. Use smart helpers like the Artificial Horizon, 4-2-6 Breathing and the 100Hz Modulation to calm down, or train your Body Stability.
