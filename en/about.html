About Me

Viativity™, an indie project from Switzerland

Hello, I'm Dieter, the developer behind Viativity™.

Viativity™ is not an app from a giant tech corporation. I am a solo developer and built this project entirely in my free time. My goal was to create an app that I would want to use myself when traveling: clearly structured, scientifically based, ad-free, and without data hunger.

As an individual, my focus is 100% on a fair product that reduces the sensory conflict of motion sickness, hides no subscription traps, and absolutely respects your privacy.

Your feedback is gold

As a solo developer, I rely on honest feedback. If Viativity™ helps you on your travels, I would be thrilled to receive a short review – you can do this easily right inside the app. Do you have suggestions, requests, or criticism? Feel free to write me a message anytime via the support link at the bottom.

Transparency & Cooperation

A modern project like Viativity™ is created today through the combination of human creativity and innovative tools. It is important to me to communicate this openly:

  • Concept & Development: The ideas, scientific research, and overall concept come from me. For the technical implementation, programming, and copy design, I was supported by artificial intelligence.
  • Design & Graphics: The illustrations and visual elements were partially generated with AI support to create a consistent, calming experience.
  • Audio Content: While the technical frequencies (like the 100Hz modulation) are generated directly in the app, the atmospheric background sounds come from the great open-source community freesound.org.
  • Localization: To make Viativity™ accessible to as many people as possible, I used artificial intelligence for translations into other languages. Since I do not speak these languages myself, the direct benefit for you was my priority – rather than a flawless translation. Please forgive any linguistic hiccups!
